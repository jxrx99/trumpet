Trump "Fake Tweet" Generator

Our team wanted to explore if we are able to predict Trump's tweet since quite a few of his tweet has been quite erratic.

Model Architecture

The model uses a bidirectional LSTM stacked with a unidirectional LSTM.

Training Methodology

A total of 13125 words were used as training data for this model. Training is done using 20 epochs

Performance

The model took approximately 15 mins to train

It achieved a loss of  6.0766 and and accuracyof 21.24% in predicting the next word after a 5 word sequence
